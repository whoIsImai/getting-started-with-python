List
    - mylist = ["apple", "banana", "cherry"]
    - ordered, changeable, and allow duplicate values, indexed.

Tuple
    - mytuple = ("apple", "banana", "cherry")
    - ordered, unchangeable, and allow duplicate values, indexed. (cannot change, add or remove items after the tuple has been created)

Set
    - myset = {"apple", "banana", "cherry"}
    - unordered, unchangeable, and do not allow duplicate values, not indexed. (unchangeable, but you can remove items and add new items)

Dictionary
    - thisdict = {"brand": "Ford", "model": "Mustang", "year": 1964}
    - ordered, changeable, and do not allow duplicates.
